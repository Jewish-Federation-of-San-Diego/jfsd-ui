Positions H2 Can Perform

H2 maps to 13 distinct staff positions across finance, development, operations, and technology. Four of these are already running as scheduled agents in the Virtual Accounting Team. The rest represent capabilities that are built and available on demand.

Each position lists what H2 handles, which systems it uses, and where human judgment remains essential. The goal is not headcount elimination. It is capacity multiplication. Staff shift from building deliverables to reviewing them, from transaction processing to exception handling.
